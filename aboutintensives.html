About our intensives

Our 2-day intensives are designed to improve your movement quality and movement magic. We have spent countless hours developing these programs to bring you the best possible education and experience.

Intensives are 8 hours spread out over 2 days. All intensives are all levels (beginner to advanced). We believe that no matter what level, you will find our intensives valuable.

MOVEMENT intensive

This technique driven intensive will help you improve your movement quality. Learn the secrets of flow and fluidity while maintaining proper positioning for safety. This intensive is ideal for anyone who wants to improve their movement quality.

MAGIC intensive

In this intensive, we will take your dance to the next level by adding emotion to your movement. Learn how to dance, and not just move! This intensive is ideal for anyone who wants to improve their stage presence and character.
